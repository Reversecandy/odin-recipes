Brownies

We have a simple brownie recipe for you choclate lovers and were gonna go over it step by step

The ingrediants you are gonna need are butter,sugar,cocoa powder,eggs,salt and vanilla,and finaly all purpose flower. Then for the tools needed it will be a mixing bowl, a small saucepan, and a spoon, and a 8 inch or similar baking pan.

  1. Melt the butter with sugar,salt and cocoa powder

    2. We will do this by taking our saucepan and filling it with a couple inches of water then put it over low heat on the stove and place our bowl filled with butter sugar and cocoa powder ove the saucepan till it turns into a gritty-looking texture.

  2. Add the vanilla and eggs

    3. After it has that texture we add one egg to the bowl of butter,sugar,and the cocoa powder and mix it then add a second egg and mix it.

  3. Add the flour

    4. Mix the flour (1/2 cup) into the batter and then beat the batter with a spoon for around 40 to 50 strokes until it is thick which will make it bake better in the oven.

  4. Bake it

    5. Put it in a 8 inch (or similar) square pan and bake for around 20 minutes. Once it gets to twenty minutes watch it and try and see when the edges look done and the middle is a little underdone to stop it. Or a simpler method is to use a timer but it might not be quite as good.

  5. Let it cool down and enjoy

    6. The timing for this will vary but once its done you have made your very own brownies.
